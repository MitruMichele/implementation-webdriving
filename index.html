Driver's License Process

1

Complete the driver's license application form online.

2

Visit VIO for driving test.

3

Pay the license fee online or at the Bank.

4

Confirm payment at State BIR Office.

5

Proceed to the FRSC Officer at the DLC for biometric data capture.

6

Visit your DLC/MLA/VIO and present your application form or temporary card to pick up your original license card.
